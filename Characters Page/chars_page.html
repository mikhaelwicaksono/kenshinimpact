Eula
"Let's see whether what I've heard of you is true. Will you indulge me in battle?"
The elegant Captain of the Ironforge Knights' Recon Squadron currently on a mission overseas.

Since her father’s death, she has been alone, rising through the ranks of the Knights by her own skill and volition. A warrior of exceptional skill, she is a name feared even by her superiors. Though she prefers to work alone, as the battles begin to get tougher, she may need to learn that allies may determine the difference between victory or death.
Dirk
"Survival may be a matter of skill or luck, and you cannot rely on luck."
The Former Chief Captain of the Ironforge Knights.

A veteran with many battles in his record, protecting and leading Cetra from the Shadows. Now in his old age, He currently resides in Gerudo Village with his adopted daughter.
Xiu Yui
“Ensure our future and protect the past.”
An aristocrat of the Yanshan Harbor's powerful Xiu Clan.

To be in the Xiu family means to collect and preserve treasures, despite resistance. However, their crusade left the common folk rejecting Yui and her family.
Ami
“Hm. Didn’t see you there. How can I be of help?”
A prodigy spirit medium residing in the icy peaks of Krasnodar Krai.

Despite having an impeccable ability to discern the supernatural and understand the physical, Yui is always a little too humble, a little too selfless. Don’t let her cold demeanor fool you, as she is a kind soul.
Barbara
“Welcome! I’m sorry, The Doctor is out of town right now, how may I help you?”
Ironforge Sector 7's resident apprentice doctor and younger sister to the acting Chief Captain.

Talk to her about herbs, medicine, politics, economy, philosophy, or life in general, then you will meet a proper young lady with deep knowledge of various things. If you talk to her about love and boys however, then you will find a human personification of a morse machine. Either way, whether it is love, biology, or medicine, she will always be there when you need her.
Jean
“I will protect everything that is dear to me. ”

The current Chief Captain of the Knights of the Great Metal City, and the first one no have any ties to the old aristocracy, as she hails from a village far away. Jean is a kind and kindred spirit, yet with a combat proficiency worth her title.

Elia
"This place looks a lot like Sunfall in 'Baron of the Bangle'! Oh, was I speaking aloud?"
A shy Knight-in-training and adopted daughter of Dirk, who took her in after her parents were killed in a Shadow raid when she was very little.

Elia has a knack for adventure stories and reads every book in the adventure genre she can find. Despite often keeping to herself, she very much has a knack for helping those around her. She also looks up to her surrogate father and has resolved to become a knight since meeting him.
Kaeya
“If it doesn’t inspire people, then what should my craft do?”
An Ironforge Knight and traveller and artist at heart, currently on a recon mission to the Krasnodar Krai region.

He comes as easy as he goes, looking to inspire and be inspired. Kaeya believes in the purity of his craft, rejecting any money that comes along with it. Though as a result, he always finds herself in strange and tricky situations just to get money to survive day by day.
