Enter Cetra, a world of knights, wizards, and spirit mediums.
The strongest among them wield the six elements: Fire, Frost, Wind, Lightning, Nuclear, and Hex against
the centuries old threat, The Shadows, brought upon a mysterious fog that comes and goes.

You play as a warrior colloquially known as ‘The Wanderer’, the lone descendant of
an extinct civilization destroyed by the Shadows millennia ago.
The Wanderer has the unique ability to wield all six elements,
along with the mysterious Illumina, a power with unknown potential.

With their unique powers, will the Ancient be able to
extinguish the fog and return light to Cetra?
